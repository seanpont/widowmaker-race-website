About The Widowmaker Invitational

The Widowmaker is more than a rock-strewn, washed-out gully running up the backbone of Mount Tamalpais. It's a Strava segment, a threat towards tired legs, a ravine so steep that only goats may pass.

On some date, a non-organized unofficial run may occur up said ravine to the summit, after which prizes will be awarded non-randomly.

Due to the narrowness of the course, as well as restrictions on the number of guys named Galen allowed on the mountain at any given time, this event is by invitation only.

Awards

The top male and female runners will each be awarded a goat.

Since most people have small apartments and non-agricultural jobs, the award is mostly symbolic. After the event, the goats will be returned to the ranch where you can visit them.
